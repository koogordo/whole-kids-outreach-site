Home Visiting Programs


Healthy Families America

WKO’s Outreach Specialists staff the Healthy Families America (HFA) Program, a nationally-recognized FREE home visiting program. It is designed to assist parents to ensure that their children receive the nurturing care that is essential to leading a healthy and productive life. Pregnant women/teens and families with infants up to 6 months old are eligible for enrollment. The Outreach Specialists can accompany parents’ on their journeys until their youngest child is three years old, providing health education, support and resources.

Maternal-Child Nursing Program

Registered nurses staff the Maternal-Child Nursing Program, which utilizes the home visiting model of Nurses for Newborns in St. Louis. The program provides a safety net for families in order to help prevent infant mortality, child abuse and neglect. In response to referrals from medical centers, physicians' offices, clinics, social service agencies and direct requests from families, Whole Kids Outreach sends specially trained nurses to the homes of pregnant women or parents.
