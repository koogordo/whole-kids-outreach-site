Web Development Team


This website was developed by students in the Computer Science Department at Winona State University. Lead developers of the website were Nick McNeely and Malek Hakim. The design and programming team also included Mohamed El-Rous, Jack Morey, Carly Nixon, Elliot Peck and Jeffrey Moon.

Students involved in the usability design and testing of the site included [Ellington team:] Ben Adelmann, Peter McPhail, Curran Rooker, Brett Tungland and Travis Walsh, and [Winona team:] Komal Bansal, Junyang Bian, Ryan DeVelder, Shelley Gorak, Nathan Karlstad, Mark Krueger, Haoxiang Li, Nathan Martin, Kyle Mohr, Shah Raiaan, Rebekah Saugen, Connor Stevens and Adam Yucuis.

The work was done as part of CS 344 in Fall 2014 and CS 444 in Spring 2015, under the direction of Professor Joan Francioni.
