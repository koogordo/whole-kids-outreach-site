Our Approach




An integrated approach for children, pregnant mothers, and familes provided by committed, knowledgable and respectful professional staff.

The focus of Whole Kids Outreach is to broadly address the many influences that place children at risk in isolated, rural, low-income areas. To enable a “whole” child to develop, WKO has created diverse programs and activities designed to educate and strengthen both the children and their parents.

Home Visit Programs

Meeting Families Where They Live

Outreach Specialists and Registered Nurses travel to their client families' homes on a regular basis as part of the Healthy Families America Program and the Maternal-Child Nursing Program.


Center-based Programs

For Kids & Parents

Center-based programs on our beautiful 10-acre property in Ellington, Missouri include a summer day camp, Moms' Day Out events, horseback riding, seasonal family festivals and more.

Overview of Programs


Home visits include health assessment and education for pregnant women and teens.


Working with horses teaches compassion & cooperation and builds self-confidence


Kids at camp learn in a fun, safe environment. Counselors learn behavior and parenting skills


Community events bring families together to play, share stories, and build connections with each other.


Families in need have a way to earn vouchers and shop for their children’s Christmas gifts.


Overburdened families with young children are offered support in their home for up to five years.
