Whole Kids Outreach works to
create strong families an ensure healthy children.

Creating strong, healthy, and happy families in rural areas of southeast Missouri.

Helping children live full lives free from preventable abuse, neglect and illness.

Helping those who don't have the means to help themselves.

Our Service Area

We serve 6 counties in rural southeast Missouri, where the average yearly income for a WKO family of four is $15,500.

Our Programs

Our diverse programs and activities are intended to help the “whole” child develop by educating and strengthening children and their parents.  

Our Children
