About Whole Kids Outreach


Our Approach

The WKO approach includes Home visits by registered nurses and outreach specialists, and Center-based programs of culturally sensitive, high-quality programming and events.

Our Team

The Whole Kids Outreach Team consists of trained nurses, outreach specialists, regional and national experts, as well as community members and volunteers.

Our History

Started in 1999
WKO integrates its programs and services to support the growth and development of pregnant mothers and children within their family structures.

WKO MISSION

Whole Kids Outreach is a faith-based, nonprofit service organization committed to the unmet health and developmental needs of children, youth, and pregnant women within their family structure.
